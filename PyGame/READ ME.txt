#Game created by Brandon Herbst

a) Kamikazee is a simple style top down shooter game, the challenge is simple, just try and shoot down as many kamikazee
pilots as possible before you blow up,
b) You have a shield and 3 lives.
c) Once all your lives are up its game over!
